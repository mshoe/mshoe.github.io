Guitar

I've been playing guitar since I was 10 years old. My first inspirations were punk and emo rock music. In high school I joined a music program where I learned classical and jazz. Near the end of highschool is when I discovered fingerstyle, which has been favorite genre to play ever since. I'm also a big fan of pop, hip hop, lofi, video game soundtracks, and film/tv soundtracks, which all play a big influence on my music. You can find many of my guitar covers and originals on my YouTube channel. If I have tabs for my original pieces, I will add them here.
