About Me

I am an M.Sc. student at Simon Fraser University advised by Prof. KangKang Yin and mentored by Prof. Xue Bin Peng. My interests are in the intersection of artificial intelligence and physics-based animation.

Previously, I received my BASc in Engineering Science from the University of Toronto in 2020, where I completed an undergraduate thesis advised by Prof. David I.W. Levin. I also spent a couple years leading the development of physical simulation software at Rocscience Inc.

Outside of work, I love playing the guitar and playing with my dog Rocky. I am also a big fan of strategy games. I grew up casually playing chess, StarCraft, WarCraft III, and Final Fantasy Tactics. More recently, I played the competitive strategy game Teamfight Tactics, ending at rank 7 out of 1,000,000+ ranked players in North America.
