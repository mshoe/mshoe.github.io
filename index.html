About Me

I am a computer science MSc student at the GrUVi Lab at Simon Fraser University, advised by Professor Xue Bin Peng. Previously, I completed my BASc in Engineering Science at the University of Toronto. I also spent a few years developing physical simulation software at Rocscience, and a summer working on generative modeling techniques for character animation at Disney Research Studios.

My research focuses on the intersection of computer animation and robotics, particularly by combining physical simulation with generative modeling and reinforcement learning.
